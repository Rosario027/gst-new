Books vs E-invoice / IMS Auto-computed

Outward supplies in Books compared with what flowed to GSTN via e-invoicing + how IMS auto-computed visibility looks to your buyers · tie-out the outward leg

← Back
Why this reconciliation matters: Every B2B invoice from registered businesses with AATO > ₹ 5 Cr must carry an IRN (e-invoice). The IRN data flows to GSTN, populates GSTR-1, and from there flows to buyers' IMS dashboards. If Books has an invoice but no IRN was generated, you'll have a GSTR-1 mismatch and your buyer can't take ITC. This reco closes that outward loop — Books invoice ↔ IRN issued ↔ IMS visible to buyer.
