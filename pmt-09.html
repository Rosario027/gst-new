PMT-09 · Transfer of amount within Cash Ledger

Move cash between major heads (IGST / CGST / SGST / Cess) or minor heads (Tax / Interest / Fee / Penalty / Other) when payment was made under the wrong head · Rule 87(13) · no refund, balance simply re-allocated

← Back to Cash Ledger
How PMT-09 works: If a cash deposit was made under the wrong head (e.g., paid CGST-Tax but should have been SGST-Tax), use PMT-09 to transfer the balance without re-depositing. Source amount must not exceed available balance in the source major+minor cell. Transfer is instant on GSTN portal · updates the electronic cash ledger immediately · does NOT trigger refund or reverse the original payment receipt.
