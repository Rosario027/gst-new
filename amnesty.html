Section 128A · Amnesty Scheme

SPL-01 / SPL-02 forms · Waiver of interest & penalty on non-fraud Section 73 demands for FY 2017-18, 2018-19 & 2019-20

About Section 128A: Inserted by Finance (No. 2) Act 2024 · effective 1-Nov-2024 (Notification 17/2024-CT). Rule 164 + FORMS SPL-01 / SPL-02 notified by Notification 20/2024-CT dated 8-Oct-2024. Covers non-fraud demands under Section 73 for FY 2017-18, 2018-19 & 2019-20. Pay tax in full by 31-Mar-2025 and apply within 3 months (30-Jun-2025) for waiver of interest and penalty.
FORM SPL-01
When SCN has been issued but no order yet passed
  • Show-cause notice (DRC-01) issued
  • Adjudicating authority has not passed order
  • You haven't filed any appeal
  • Demand is under Section 73 (not §74 fraud)
FORM SPL-02
When order has been passed (DRC-07 / 08 / APL-04)
  • Order in original passed (DRC-07 / 08)
  • Or appeal order issued (APL-04)
  • Tax has been paid in full
  • You will withdraw any pending appeal
