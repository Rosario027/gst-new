DRC-03A · Link voluntary payment to demand order

Adjust an earlier DRC-03 voluntary payment against a subsequent demand · Rule 142(2B) · Notification 12/2024-CT

What DRC-03A does: Earlier, taxpayers who paid tax voluntarily via DRC-03 and were later issued a demand order (DRC-07 / DRC-08 / MOV-09 / MOV-11 / APL-04) for the same liability had to pay again — the DRC-03 payment did not auto-offset against the demand. DRC-03A (available from Nov 2024) lets you formally link the earlier voluntary payment to the demand order so the electronic liability register marks it as discharged.
