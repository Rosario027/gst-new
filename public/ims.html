About credit note treatment
Rejecting a credit note historically added value back to the supplier's output liability — a treatment industry has been petitioning the GST Council to relax. FylePro treats this as configuration-driven and pulls the latest GSTN advisory at runtime. Check your organisation settings for the current policy applied.
