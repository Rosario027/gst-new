GSTR-2A vs GSTR-2B

Dynamic supplier ledger (2A) compared with the static 28th-of-month snapshot (2B) · gaps surface late-filed invoices, supplier amendments, and post-cut-off activity

← Back
Why this reconciliation matters: GSTR-2A is dynamic · it updates whenever any supplier files / amends a GSTR-1 or GSTR-1A. GSTR-2B is a frozen snapshot taken on the 14th. If a supplier files between 14th and 20th, the invoice appears in 2A but not in the 2B that drives your ITC eligibility. This reco surfaces that gap so you can plan ITC carry-forward to next period.
