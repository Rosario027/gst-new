E-Way Bill (EWB)

Mandatory for movement of goods > ₹ 50,000 · generated on the dedicated EWB portal · validity 1 day per 200 km · cancellation window 24h post-generation

+ New EWB
EWB vs IRN — what's different: IRN is for the invoice itself (B2B > ₹ 5 Cr), one-time, never expires, generated on IRP. EWB is for the movement of goods, mandatory whenever consignment value > ₹ 50k, has a strict validity period (1 day / 200 km), can be cancelled within 24 hours, and is generated on a separate portal (ewaybillgst.gov.in). One invoice may have 0, 1, or several EWBs depending on how the goods are shipped.
