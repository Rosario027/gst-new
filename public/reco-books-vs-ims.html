Why this reconciliation matters: IMS is the new (Oct-2024) workflow layer between supplier filings and your 2B. Each IMS record must be Accepted, Rejected, or kept Pending. Books vs IMS reconciliation tells you which IMS records align with what you've actually booked — making the Accept/Reject decision data-driven instead of a guess. Un-actioned IMS records default to Accepted at 2B generation.
