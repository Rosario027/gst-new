Allowed in GSTR-1A
  • Add missed invoices for the same period
  • Amend taxable value, tax rate, IGST/CGST/SGST
  • Amend Place of Supply
  • Amend HSN, invoice date (within FY)
  • Add credit / debit notes
NOT allowed
  • Changing recipient GSTIN
  • Amending records from earlier periods
  • Filing more than one GSTR-1A per period
  • Changing export indicator (with/without payment)
Timing rules
  • Available after GSTR-1 is filed
  • Must file before GSTR-3B
  • QRMP: one per quarter
  • Monthly: one per month
