Why this reconciliation matters: Suppliers file GSTR-1 for invoices they raised on you; that data flows into your 2A. Your purchase register (Books) is what you booked from the same suppliers. They should match invoice-by-invoice. Missing rows on either side indicate either an unreported supplier filing or an unrecorded purchase — both require investigation before ITC claim.
