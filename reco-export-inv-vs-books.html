Why this reconciliation matters: Export invoices need to tie across three systems for IGST refund to release: (1) Books / Sales register, (2) e-invoice IRN with EXP type and shipping bill no, (3) Shipping Bill in ICEGATE customs. The refund engine cross-references all three — any mismatch holds the refund. Even a 1-day date difference between IRN and Shipping Bill stalls the claim.
